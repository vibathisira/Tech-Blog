1.Do I need to pay to read articles on this blog?

NO.All technical article,tutorials,and news publised on Tech Blog are completely free to read and learn from

2. Can I contribute and write technical articles for this blog?

Yes, absolutely! If you are into Software Engineering or working in the tech industry, you can connect with us through our "Join Us" page and submit your articles.

3. Are these articles suitable for beginners learning Programming and Git/GitHub?

Yes, they are. Our main goal is to explain complex technical topics in a very simple and easy-to-understand manner. Therefore, the content is well-structured even for absolute beginners.

4. Can I unsubscribe from the weekly newsletter at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your newsletter subscription whenever you want by simply clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email you receive from us.

5. How can I discuss technical issues or ask questions?

There is a comment section at the end of every article. If you post your problem there, our team or someone from the community will be happy to help you out.
